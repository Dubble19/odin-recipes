Burritos

Description

These burritos are jam packed with flavour and the calories that you will want as a hard gainer. Donnt get me wrong the preperation time is a little longer that what I would usually cook, but they are more than worth the wait.
Just about breaking the 1000 calorie barrier (once again to your preference of additions) This a great evening meal to keep you full till bed time.

Ingredients

  • 10% Mince: 450g
  • Kidney beans: 1 tin
  • White Wraps: 3
  • Chilli con carne: 1 jar
  • Granishes: curry powder, salt, pepper, paprika

Steps

  1. Cook the mince in a pan until brown
  2. Add the garnishes once fully cooked
  3. Add the sauce to the pan
  4. Add kidney beans and stir together
  5. Split the filling evenly between the 3 wraps place in the air fryer for 3-4 minutes
