Yoghurt Bowl

Description

Now this is a recipe that has been dear to my heart for some time. Whenever I am low on time and need a calorific breakfast, this is my go to choice. It boasts over 800 calories with all natural ingredients, depending on your protein powder of course.
This is something that for myself has become a regular breakfast 5-6 days a week as its easy to eat, easy to package and take with me to work. It takes less than 5 minutes to prepare this meal which is grat for someone like me who likes spending as
little time in the kitchen as I can when it comes to prepping my meals.

Ingredients

  • Yoghurt: 5 tbsp's
  • Peanut Butter: 1 tbsp
  • Protein Powder: 1 scoop
  • Berry Granola: 60g

Steps

  1. Place all of youghurt into the bowl
  2. Add your peanut butter
  3. Add the protein poweder on top
  4. Sprinkle the granola evenly on top
  5. Stir until it becomes a wt and sticky subtance and all of your powder is mixed in
